[European Commission](https://commission.europa.eu/index_en) has a large amount
of various information sources.

This dataset serves the purpose to simplify dataaccess and aggregation.

## What's included

The dataset covers daily updates of:

- Participant register (EU Funding & Tenders)
- CORDIS containing Horizon Europe, Horizon H2020 and FP7 projects

## How to gain access

To access this dataset the user must have claims includling both ica and ica-catalog.
